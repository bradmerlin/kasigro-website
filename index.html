Nge Kasigro

Yinkqubo eqalwa liqembu lase Germany kunye nase Mzantsi Afrika ephathisana noosomashishini basekuhlaleni. Siqala ngoqwalasela abenzi bee nwele.

Iqembu le Kasigro liyakholelwa kwikhono elinikwa ifuthe nempembelelo yawo wonke umnntu oqhuba ishishini elimileyo emphakathini.

Sisonke sibambisene izandla nabaphathi bendawo kunye noosomashishini bomphakathi abamileyo, sizimisele ekwakheni inkqubo' ntlalo yamashishini eyokwenza kube lula ukwakha amashishini esekuhlaleni aphumelelayo.
