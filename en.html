About Kasigro

Kasigro is a German-South African initiative collaborating with entrepreneurs in townships. Initially, we are focusing on hair salons.>

The Kasigro Team believes in this industry's tremendous potential, and we are inspired by the capability of every individual entrepreneur who has already established their business.

Together with the key stakeholders and experienced local micro-entrepreneurs, we aim to shape an ecosystem that makes it easier for informal businesses to thrive.
